# Privacy Policy for Athi Live Echo Mic

**Last Updated:** July 4, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use Athi Live Echo Mic ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.** 
Live Echo Mic is designed with a privacy-first architecture. All audio processing (including echo, reverb, and chorus effects) is performed **100% locally on your device** in real-time. 

- **Microphone Access:** We request microphone access solely to process the audio through our DSP (Digital Signal Processing) engine. No audio data is ever recorded, saved to your device storage, or transmitted over the internet.
- **No Analytics or Tracking:** We do not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking software.
- **No Advertisements:** Our app is completely ad-free. We do not integrate ad networks or share any device identifiers.

## 2. Third-Party Services
Since we do not transmit any data, we do not share your information with any third-party services, advertisers, or affiliates.

## 3. Children's Privacy
Our application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
